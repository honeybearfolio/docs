## About the Project

**HoneyBear Folio** is a modern, cross-platform personal finance desktop application designed for privacy, speed, and comprehensive financial tracking. Built with **Tauri** and **React**, it offers a fast, native-feeling experience while keeping your data strictly local on your device in a secure SQLite database.

We believe your financial data belongs to you. That's why HoneyBear Folio operates completely offline-first without requiring remote servers or account registrations.

### Why HoneyBear Folio?

- **Privacy First**: Your data never leaves your machine unless you explicitly export it.
- **Advanced Tracking**: Monitor your net worth, track investment portfolios with real-time quotes, and analyze income vs. expenses.
- **FIRE Projections**: Plan your future with built-in Financial Independence, Retire Early (FIRE) calculators.
- **Powerful Automation**: Use regex-based auto-fill rules to categorize transactions automatically.
- **Multi-Currency**: Seamlessly handle accounts and transactions in different currencies with automatic rate fetching.

Whether you are tracking daily expenses or managing a complex investment portfolio, HoneyBear Folio provides the tools you need without sacrificing privacy or usability.
